I am currently a Lecturer and Master's supervisor at the School of Computer and Communication Engineering, Northeastern University at Qinhuangdao. I received my Ph.D. degree in Cyberspace Security from the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Wuhan University, in December 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohui Cui.

My research interests include machine learning, data mining, generative models, imbalanced learning, object detection and segmentation, and multimodal learning. My current research focuses on leveraging deep generative models to address data imbalance and small-sample challenges in machine learning, thereby improving the performance of downstream classification and segmentation tasks.

Since 2025, I have been recruiting Master's and undergraduate students, and I welcome motivated students to join my group.
